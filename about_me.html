MY STORY

MY CAMERA + ME

Welcome! I am a passionate photographer specializing in various genres. My journey in photography started from a young age, and I have developed a keen eye for capturing moments that tell a story. I love working with clients to create beautiful memories that last a lifetime.

I am a professional wedding photographer in Pretoria. I also specialise in other types of photography such as pregnancy shoots, birthdays, couple engagements, music live performances, etc. I am a proud husband and father. I am also a musician at heart. I studied Human Resource as a profession. Photography started as a hobby and I later turned it into a business after I received a lot of praise and encouragement from people who viewed my photography work. Through passion and consistency I found growth in the art within a short space of time and I plan in growing further. My style of wedding photography is very unique and always comes with something extra. I believe in capturing people’s special moments in its purest form and in a way that tells a story like a never ending movie.
